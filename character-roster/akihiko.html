Akihiko Sanada

"I'm not holding back!"

Backstory

Akihiko Sanada is a dedicated boxer and a key member of SEES. With his Persona, Caesar, he enhances his lightning-infused punches to dominate opponents in close combat.

Persona: Caesar

Caesar amplifies Akihiko’s raw strength, granting him high-speed melee attacks and powerful electric-based strikes.

Character Stats

  • Role: Striker (DPS)
  • Max SP: 230
  • Mobility: High
  • Primary Attribute: Electric Damage

Moveset & SP Costs

  • Basic Attack (R2): Lightning-infused punch combos. (No SP)
  • Holding R2: Powerful haymaker that stuns enemies. (No SP, Knockback Effect)
  • L2 - Quick Sidestep: Evades incoming attacks with a short dash.
  • Triangle - Thunder Uppercut: Electrified uppercut attack. (30 SP)
  • L1 - Counter Hook: Parries and counters with a powerful punch. (40 SP)
  • R1 - Lightning Barrage: Rapid flurry of electric punches. (50 SP)
  • Ultimate - Caesar’s Storm: Akihiko unleashes a storm of electric-infused punches, finishing with a powerful cross. (150 SP)
