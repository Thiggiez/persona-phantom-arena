Goro Akechi

"Justice is an illusion, but power is absolute."

Backstory

Akechi is a charismatic detective prodigy who hides a vengeful and chaotic nature. Using his Personas, Robin Hood and Loki, he balances precision gunplay with devastating Curse magic.

Moveset & SP Costs

Robin Hood Form

  • Basic Attack (R2): Slashes with his sword. (No SP)
  • Holding R2: Fires his laser pistol. (6 shots, Ammo-based)
  • L2 - Phantom Step: Quick evasive movement. (No SP, Short Cooldown)
  • Triangle - Persona Swap: Press Triangle to switch to Loki when Madness is full.
  • L1 - Kougaon: Fires a concentrated Bless attack. (30 SP)
  • R1 - Triple Shot: Fires three rapid shots from his pistol. (No SP, Ammo-based)
  • Ultimate - Heavenly Judgement: Summons Robin Hood to unleash a massive Bless explosion. (160 SP)

Loki Form (Madness Mode)

  • Basic Attack (R2): Slashes wildly with dual daggers. (No SP)
  • Holding R2: Throws multiple cursed daggers. (No SP, Limited Uses)
  • L2 - Phantom Step: Becomes invulnerable briefly during dodge. (No SP, Short Cooldown)
  • L1 - Eigaon: Fires a powerful Curse projectile. (40 SP)
  • R1 - Megidolaon: Almighty explosion in an area. (80 SP)
  • Ultimate - Ragnarok Descent: Summons Loki for a massive multi-hit Curse strike. (180 SP)
