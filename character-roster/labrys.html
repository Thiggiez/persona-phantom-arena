Labrys

"I'm more than just a weapon!"

Backstory

Labrys is a battle android created for combat but fights to claim her own identity. With her Persona, Ariadne, she wields a massive axe to deliver devastating physical attacks.

Persona: Ariadne

Ariadne enhances Labrys' raw power, allowing her to deliver heavy strikes and control enemies with chains.

Character Stats

  • Role: Striker (DPS)
  • Max SP: 230
  • Mobility: Medium
  • Primary Attribute: Physical Damage

Moveset & SP Costs

  • Basic Attack (R2): Swings her massive axe. (No SP)
  • Holding R2: Charges up for an Executioner’s Blow. (No SP, Heavy Knockback)
  • L2 - Dash Boost: Performs a short burst forward to reposition.
  • Triangle - Power Charge: Temporarily boosts the damage of the next attack. (30 SP)
  • L1 - Guillotine Axe: A massive overhead slash that cleaves enemies. (40 SP)
  • R1 - Chain Knuckle: Sends out chains to pull enemies closer. (50 SP)
  • Ultimate - Ariadne’s Wrath: Labrys delivers a spinning axe strike that sends shockwaves outward. (150 SP)
