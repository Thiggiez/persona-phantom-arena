Joker - Persona Phantom Arena

"I am thou, thou art I..."

Backstory

A high school student falsely accused of a crime, Joker becomes the leader of the Phantom Thieves.

Persona: Arsène

Arsène specializes in curse-based attacks and swift, deceptive strikes.

Character Stats

  • Role: Striker (DPS)
  • Max HP: 250
  • Max SP: 250
  • Mobility: High
  • Primary Attribute: Curse Magic

Moveset & SP Costs

  • Basic Attack (R2): Slashes with his dagger. (No SP)
  • Holding R2: Fires his pistol. (6 shots, Ammo-based)
  • L2 - Phantom Step: Quickly dodges in any direction. (No SP, Short Cooldown)
  • Triangle - Persona Swap: Holds Triangle + L1/R1 to switch Personas. (No SP)
  • L1 - Eiha: Shoots a small Curse projectile. (30 SP)
  • R1 - Dream Needle: Small damage with a chance to put enemies to sleep. (50 HP Cost)
  • Ultimate - Satanael: Joker summons Satanael for a devastating Almighty attack. (150 SP)
