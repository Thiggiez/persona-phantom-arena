Sophia

"I am humanity's companion!"

Backstory

Sophia is an AI with a kind heart and a desire to understand humanity. With her Persona, Pandora, she utilizes yo-yo-based combat and light magic to assist her allies.

Persona: Pandora

Pandora grants Sophia the ability to use light-based magic and long-range yo-yo strikes.

Character Stats

  • Role: Striker (DPS)
  • Max SP: 240
  • Mobility: High
  • Primary Attribute: Bless Damage

Moveset & SP Costs

  • Basic Attack (R2): Strikes with her yo-yos. (No SP)
  • Holding R2: Unleashes a flurry of yo-yo strikes. (No SP)
  • L2 - Teleport: Instantly moves to a new location.
  • Triangle - Pandora’s Light: Sends out energy waves dealing Bless damage. (35 SP)
  • L1 - Blessing Field: Allies slowly regenerate HP over time. (40 SP)
  • R1 - Chain Combo: Multi-hit yo-yo strikes that trap enemies. (50 SP)
  • Ultimate - Pandora’s Awakening: Creates a massive light explosion, damaging enemies in a wide area. (150 SP)
