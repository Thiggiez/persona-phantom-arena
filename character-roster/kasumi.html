Kasumi Yoshizawa

"I will dance my way to victory!"

Backstory

Kasumi Yoshizawa is an elite gymnast who joins the Phantom Thieves with a determination to uphold justice. With her Persona, Cendrillon, she delivers graceful yet deadly sword attacks imbued with Bless energy.

Persona: Cendrillon

Cendrillon enhances Kasumi’s agility and finesse, allowing for swift, high-flying acrobatics and Bless-infused slashes.

Character Stats

  • Role: Striker (DPS)
  • Max SP: 250
  • Mobility: Very High
  • Primary Attribute: Bless Damage

Moveset & SP Costs

  • Basic Attack (R2): Elegant saber strikes. (No SP)
  • Holding R2: Performs an acrobatic twirl, slashing multiple times. (No SP)
  • L2 - Phantom Step: Performs a quick aerial dash.
  • Triangle - Bless Strike: Sends out a radiant sword slash. (30 SP)
  • L1 - Divine Grace: Temporarily boosts attack speed. (40 SP)
  • R1 - Acrobat Combo: Aerial spin into a downward strike. (50 SP)
  • Ultimate - Cendrillon’s Finale: Kasumi ascends mid-air and unleashes a massive, elegant Bless-infused slash. (150 SP)
