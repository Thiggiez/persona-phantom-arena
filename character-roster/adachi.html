Tohru Adachi

"Heh... you never saw this coming, huh?"

Backstory

Tohru Adachi is a cunning and unpredictable former detective with a twisted sense of justice. Wielding his Persona, Magatsu-Izanagi, he manipulates dark energy and deceives enemies with his erratic fighting style.. He’s quick-witted and even quicker on his feet, using his Persona, Jiraiya, to unleash rapid wind-based strikes and swift melee combos. with dreams of being a hero. he takes down enemies with overwhelming strength and unpredictable attacks.

Persona: Magatsu-Izanagi

Magatsu-Izanagi enhances Adachi’s control over dark energy, allowing him to fire deadly projectiles while confusing and overwhelming his foes., allowing him to rapidly strike foes with kunai while controlling the battlefield with wind magic. allowing him to excel at both melee combat and magical offense.

Character Stats

  • Role: Striker (DPS)
  • Max SP: 200
  • Mobility: Low
  • Primary Attribute: Dark & Gun Damage

Moveset & SP Costs

  • Basic Attack (R2): Fires his revolver in quick succession. (No SP, Ammo-based)
  • Holding R2: Fires a charged shot that pierces enemies. (No SP, Requires Reload)
  • L2 - Shadow Step: Teleports a short distance, leaving a shadow decoy behind. (No SP, Short Cooldown)
  • Triangle - Magatsu Mandala: Fires a burst of dark energy, inflicting debuffs on enemies. (40 SP)
  • L1 - Evil Smile: Inflicts Fear on nearby enemies, making them vulnerable. (30 SP)
  • R1 - Agidyne: Launches a large fireball at enemies. (50 SP)
  • Ultimate - Magatsu Chaos: Adachi unleashes a wave of dark energy, causing massive AoE damage and applying Fear to all enemies hit. (160 SP)
