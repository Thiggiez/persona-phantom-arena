Yosuke Hanamura

"Let's do this, partner!"

Backstory

Yosuke Hanamura is the energetic best friend of the Investigation Team. He’s quick-witted and even quicker on his feet, using his Persona, Jiraiya, to unleash rapid wind-based strikes and swift melee combos. with dreams of being a hero. he takes down enemies with overwhelming strength and unpredictable attacks.

Persona: Jiraiya

Jiraiya enhances Yosuke’s speed and agility, allowing him to rapidly strike foes with kunai while controlling the battlefield with wind magic. allowing him to excel at both melee combat and magical offense.

Character Stats

  • Role: Striker (DPS)
  • Max SP: 200
  • Mobility: Low
  • Primary Attribute: Wind & Melee Damage

Moveset & SP Costs

  • Basic Attack (R2): Slashes rapidly with his dual kunai. (No SP)
  • Holding R2: Throws kunai at enemies for light ranged damage. (No SP, Limited Uses: 5 kunai per refill)
  • L2 - Phantom Step: Quickly dashes in any direction, covering more distance than most. (No SP, Short Cooldown)
  • Triangle - Garudyne: Unleashes a powerful wind slash that travels forward, damaging enemies in a straight line. (35 SP)
  • L1 - Sukukaja: Increases Yosuke’s movement speed and attack speed for 8 seconds. (40 SP, Cooldown: 12s)
  • R1 - Gale Strike: Yosuke performs a spinning aerial slash that hits all enemies around him. (50 SP, Can be used mid-air)
  • Ultimate - Hurricane Frenzy: Yosuke calls upon Jiraiya to summon a massive cyclone, trapping enemies in a vortex of slicing wind attacks before sending them flying. (150 SP, Large AoE)
