Junpei Iori

"I'm Junpei Iori, Ace Detective of the Phantom Thieves!"

Backstory

Junpei Iori is a fun-loving, energetic member of SEES with dreams of being a hero. Wielding a powerful bat and channeling fire magic through his Persona, Trismegistus, he takes down enemies with overwhelming strength and unpredictable attacks.. Though rough around the edges, he cares deeply for his friends and will fight to protect them. Wielding a massive axe and powered by his Persona, Castor, he is a devastating force on the battlefield.

Persona: Trismegistus

Trismegistus grants Junpei powerful fire abilities and immense physical strength, allowing him to excel at both melee combat and magical offense., allowing him to unleash brutal axe attacks while enduring heavy damage.

Character Stats

  • Role: Striker (DPS)
  • Max SP: 200
  • Mobility: Low
  • Primary Attribute: Fire & Melee Damage

Moveset & SP Costs

  • Basic Attack (R2): Swings his baseball bat in a quick arc. (No SP)
  • Holding R2: Home-run swing for extra damage. (No SP)
  • L2 - Dash Step: Dashes forward to reposition quickly. (No SP, Short Cooldown)
  • Triangle - Agidyne: Launches a massive fireball at enemies. (40 SP)
  • L1 - Home Run Buff: Temporarily boosts Junpei’s attack speed. (30 SP)
  • R1 - Rebellion: Increases Junpei’s critical hit rate for a short time. (35 SP)
  • Ultimate - Trismegistus’ Inferno: Junpei summons Trismegistus to unleash a fiery explosion, dealing massive fire damage in a large area. (170 SP)
