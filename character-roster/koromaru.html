Koromaru

"Woof!"

Backstory

Koromaru is a loyal and intelligent Shiba Inu who fights alongside SEES. With his Persona, Cerberus, he delivers swift and deadly fire-based attacks.

Persona: Cerberus

Cerberus grants Koromaru high-speed movement and powerful fire-based abilities.

Character Stats

  • Role: Striker (DPS)
  • Max SP: 220
  • Mobility: Very High
  • Primary Attribute: Fire Damage

Moveset & SP Costs

  • Basic Attack (R2): Slashes with his dagger. (No SP)
  • Holding R2: Throws his dagger for ranged attacks. (No SP, Limited Uses)
  • L2 - Quick Dash: Performs a lightning-fast dash.
  • Triangle - Agilao: Fires a mid-range fireball. (30 SP)
  • L1 - Sukukaja: Temporarily increases Koromaru’s movement speed. (40 SP)
  • R1 - Counter Stance: Enters a stance that automatically counters melee attacks. (50 SP)
  • Ultimate - Cerberus’ Infernal Blaze: Koromaru and Cerberus unleash a devastating fire explosion. (150 SP)
