Naoto Shirogane

"The truth is always within our grasp."

Backstory

Naoto Shirogane, the "Detective Prince," is a prodigy investigator who joins the Investigation Team to solve the mysteries plaguing Inaba. Using her Persona, Sukuna-Hikona, she specializes in precise ranged combat and Almighty attacks.

Persona: Sukuna-Hikona

Sukuna-Hikona grants Naoto unparalleled gunplay and supernatural abilities, focusing on **Almighty** and **status-inflicting** attacks.

Character Stats

  • Role: Striker (DPS)
  • Max SP: 240
  • Mobility: High
  • Primary Attribute: Almighty Damage

Moveset & SP Costs

  • Basic Attack (R2): Shoots her revolver. (No SP)
  • Holding R2: Precision shot. (No SP, Ammo-based)
  • L2 - Aim Mode: Allows Naoto to aim manually for more precise shots.
  • Triangle - Megidolaon: Almighty explosion dealing AoE damage. (50 SP)
  • L1 - Sukunda: Lowers enemy evasion for a short time. (30 SP)
  • R1 - Swift Reload: Instantly reloads Naoto’s revolver. (No SP, Short Cooldown)
  • Ultimate - Yamato-Takeru’s Final Barrage: Unleashes a rapid-fire almighty attack, finishing with a high-powered shot. (120 SP)
