Shinjiro Aragaki

"If you wanna act tough, you better be ready to back it up."

Backstory

Once a senior member of SEES, Shinjiro left the group to deal with personal demons. Though rough around the edges, he cares deeply for his friends and will fight to protect them. Wielding a massive axe and powered by his Persona, Castor, he is a devastating force on the battlefield.

Persona: Castor

Castor grants Shinjiro immense strength and resilience, allowing him to unleash brutal axe attacks while enduring heavy damage.

Character Stats

  • Role: Striker (DPS)
  • Max SP: 200
  • Mobility: Low
  • Primary Attribute: Heavy Melee & Physical Attacks

Moveset & SP Costs

  • Basic Attack (R2): Swings his heavy axe. (No SP)
  • Holding R2: Executioner’s Blow – A massive downward cleave. (No SP)
  • L2 - Armor Stance: Briefly reduces incoming damage. (No SP, Short Cooldown)
  • Triangle - Fatal Impact: Leaps and smashes the ground, causing a shockwave. (40 SP)
  • L1 - Berserk: Boosts attack power but reduces defense. (30 SP)
  • R1 - Axe Cleave: Swings in a wide arc, hitting multiple enemies. (35 SP)
  • Ultimate - Castor’s Do or Die: Shinjiro enters a berserk state, greatly increasing attack speed and lifesteal for a short time. (170 SP)
