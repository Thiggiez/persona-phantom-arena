Shinjiro Aragaki

"...I don't have time for regrets."

Backstory

Once a senior member of SEES, Shinjiro left the group to deal with personal demons. Returning to protect those he cares for, he wields his Persona, Castor, and devastates enemies with brute force.

Persona: Castor

Castor grants Shinjiro immense strength and resilience, allowing him to overpower foes with heavy melee attacks.

Character Stats

  • Role: Striker (DPS)
  • Max SP: 200
  • Mobility: Low
  • Primary Attribute: Heavy Melee & Physical Attacks

Moveset & SP Costs

  • Basic Attack (R2): Swings his heavy axe in a slow but powerful arc. (No SP)
  • Holding R2: Executioner’s Blow - A massive downward axe swing. (No SP, Knockback Effect)
  • L2 - Short Dash: Dashes forward while gaining armor. (No SP, Short Cooldown)
  • Triangle - Fatal Impact: Leaps into the air and slams his axe down. (40 SP)
  • L1 - Berserk: Increases Shinjiro’s attack power but reduces defense. (30 SP)
  • R1 - Axe Cleave: Swings his axe in a wide arc, hitting multiple enemies. (50 SP)
  • Ultimate - Do or Die: Shinjiro enters a berserk state, massively increasing attack speed and lifesteal for a short time. (170 SP)
