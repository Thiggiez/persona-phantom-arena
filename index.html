Persona Phantom Arena

About the Game

Persona Phantom Arena is a fan-made hero shooter inspired by the Persona franchise. Players take on the roles of their favorite Persona characters, each with unique abilities and Personas, in fast-paced, tactical battles. Team up, strategize, and unleash powerful Persona attacks in a visually stunning arena.

Persona Phantom Arena is a fan project and is not affiliated with Atlus or Sega. Persona is a trademark of Atlus and Sega.
